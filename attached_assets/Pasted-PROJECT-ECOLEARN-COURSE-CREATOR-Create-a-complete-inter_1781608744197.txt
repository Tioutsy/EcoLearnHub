PROJECT: ECOLEARN COURSE CREATOR

Create a complete interactive course inside EcoLearn called:

"Sustainability Foundations"

The course should be designed for all employee types regardless of role, industry or education level.

Target completion time:
15-20 minutes.

Primary objective:
Help employees understand sustainability and identify practical actions they can implement immediately in the workplace.

The course should be engaging, visual, interactive and built using micro-learning principles.

---

COURSE STRUCTURE

Module 1: Welcome to Sustainability

Learning Objective:
Understand what sustainability means.

Content:

* Short introduction
* Interactive scenario
* Visual illustrations
* One knowledge check

Quiz:
1 multiple choice question.

---

Module 2: Why Sustainability Matters

Learning Objective:
Understand business and environmental benefits.

Content:

* Short explainer
* Interactive matching exercise

Topics:

* Waste reduction
* Energy savings
* Water conservation
* Corporate responsibility

Quiz:
2 questions.

---

Module 3: Sustainability in Mauritius

Learning Objective:
Connect sustainability to local realities.

Topics:

* Waste management
* Plastic pollution
* Water scarcity
* Environmental challenges

Add:

* Mauritian case study section
* Visual storytelling

Quiz:
2 questions.

---

Module 4: Your Role as an Employee

Learning Objective:
Understand personal responsibility.

Content:
Role-specific examples:

* Office employee
* Receptionist
* Technician
* Housekeeper
* Manager

Interactive activity:
Select actions you can implement.

---

Module 5: Everyday Sustainability Actions

Learning Objective:
Learn practical workplace actions.

Topics:

* Energy
* Water
* Waste
* Paper
* Transport

Create:
Interactive decision-making scenarios.

Example:
"What would you do if you noticed recyclables being placed in the wrong bin?"

---

Module 6: Sustainability Commitment

Learning Objective:
Convert learning into action.

Employee selects:

* Reduce waste
* Save water
* Save energy
* Recycle correctly
* Encourage colleagues

Store selections in database.

Allow future tracking.

---

FINAL QUIZ

10 questions maximum.

Passing score:
80%

Allow retakes.

Display results immediately.

---

CERTIFICATE SYSTEM

Generate PDF certificate automatically upon successful completion.

Certificate must include:

* Employee name
* Company name
* Course name
* Completion date
* Unique certificate number
* QR verification code

---

BADGE SYSTEM

Award:

"Sustainability Starter"

Display badge on employee profile.

Store badge in database.

---

PROGRESSION SYSTEM

Display:

Course completion percentage

Modules completed

Quiz score

Certificate earned

Badge earned

---

NEXT COURSE RECOMMENDATION

After completion show:

"Recommended Next Course:
Waste Sorting & The Mauritian Bin System"

---

GAMIFICATION

Award:

* Points per module
* Bonus points for perfect quiz scores
* Completion badge

Display points in employee dashboard.

---

USER EXPERIENCE REQUIREMENTS

* Mobile-friendly
* Modern visual design
* Short content blocks
* Maximum 3 minutes reading per section
* Progress indicators
* Interactive learning
* No large text walls
* High completion rate focus

The course should feel like an engaging learning experience rather than corporate compliance training.

The tone should be practical, positive and action-oriented.
