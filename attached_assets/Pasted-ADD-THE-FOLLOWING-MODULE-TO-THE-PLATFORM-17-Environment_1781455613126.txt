17. Environmental Impact & Sustainability Dashboard

Create a dedicated Environmental Impact Dashboard for each company.

The objective is to help companies visualize the positive impact of employee training and demonstrate their commitment to sustainability and ESG objectives.

The dashboard should include:

* Number of employees trained
* Number of certificates issued
* Training completion rate
* Number of active learners
* Number of departments trained

Environmental impact indicators (estimated values based on company data and industry benchmarks):

* Estimated kilograms of recyclable waste diverted from landfill
* Estimated reduction in mixed waste contamination
* Estimated reduction in single-use plastics
* Estimated increase in recycling participation rates
* Estimated CO₂ emissions avoided
* Environmental score generated by the platform

Visual reporting:

* Interactive charts
* Monthly and annual comparisons
* Progress tracking over time
* Department-by-department performance comparison

Management Reports:

* Downloadable PDF Sustainability Report
* Downloadable ESG Training Report
* Annual Environmental Awareness Report
* Executive Summary for Management Teams

Certification & Recognition Program:

The platform should automatically award digital badges based on employee participation and company performance, such as:

* Eco-Aware Company
* Recycling Champion
* Sustainable Workplace
* Green Office Partner
* Environmental Leadership Award

Company Sustainability Profile Page:

Each subscribing company receives a public profile page displaying:

* Number of employees trained
* Sustainability achievements
* Environmental badges obtained
* Commitment to responsible waste management

This page can be shared on social media, websites, annual reports, and ESG disclosures.

Business Benefits Section:

The platform should clearly communicate measurable business benefits:

* Improved ESG performance
* Easier compliance reporting
* Better employee engagement
* Stronger environmental culture
* Enhanced corporate image
* Reduced environmental risks
* Support for sustainability certifications

Future Enhancement:

Allow integration with waste collection and recycling service providers in Mauritius so companies can combine:

* Employee training
* Waste tracking
* Recycling performance monitoring
* Sustainability reporting

This creates a complete environmental management ecosystem rather than simply an online training platform.
