PROJECT: EVOLVE ECOLEARN INTO MAURITIUS' LEADING CORPORATE SUSTAINABILITY & ESG TRAINING PLATFORM

CONTEXT

EcoLearn is no longer simply an e-learning platform. It must evolve into a complete corporate sustainability training and reporting solution for Mauritian businesses.

Our target customers are:

* SMEs
* Hotels
* Property Management Companies
* Syndics / Co-Ownership Managers
* Real Estate Developers
* Manufacturing Companies
* Corporate Offices
* Educational Institutions

The primary buyer is NOT the employee.

The primary buyer is:

* CEO
* HR Manager
* ESG Manager
* Operations Manager
* Compliance Manager

The platform must therefore focus on:

1. Employee training
2. Progress tracking
3. Reporting
4. Compliance support
5. ESG engagement
6. Sustainability metrics

The objective is to make EcoLearn indispensable for companies wanting to demonstrate sustainability engagement.

PHASE 2 - ESG IMPACT DASHBOARD

Create a new section called:

"Sustainability Impact"

Display estimated impact generated through training and employee engagement.

Examples:

* Waste diverted from landfill
* Recycling participation
* Plastic reduction score
* Water savings awareness score
* Carbon awareness score

Include:

* Trees equivalent saved
* CO2 equivalent impact
* Sustainability engagement score

Even if values are calculated through predefined formulas and assumptions.

Make the dashboard highly visual.

The goal is to give companies meaningful ESG metrics.
