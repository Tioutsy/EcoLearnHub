Create a professional e-learning web platform called “EcoLearn Mauritius” dedicated to waste sorting, recycling, sustainability, environmental awareness, and corporate environmental responsibility in Mauritius.

The platform’s objective is to allow companies to train employees quickly, efficiently, and remotely without requiring physical workshops or travel.

Target clients include:

* Hotels and resorts
* Offices and corporate businesses
* Property management companies
* Co-ownership management companies (syndics)
* Shopping centres
* Manufacturing companies
* Schools and educational institutions
* ESG-conscious businesses

The platform must include:

1. Homepage

* Modern, professional and environmentally focused design.
* Highlight the benefits of online training:
    * Train employees anytime and anywhere.
    * No travel required.
    * Reduced training costs.
    * Standardized training quality.
    * Automatic certification.
    * Compliance and ESG support.
* Call-to-action buttons:
    * Browse Courses
    * Purchase Training
    * Corporate Packages
    * Contact Us

2. Course Marketplace
    Display all available courses with:

* Course title
* Description
* Learning objectives
* Duration
* Preview lesson
* Price
* Certificate included

3. Course Categories
    Create courses within:

* Introduction to Sustainability
* Waste Sorting in Mauritius
* Recycling Best Practices
* Plastic Reduction Strategies
* Office Sustainability
* Environmental Responsibilities of Employees
* Sustainable Hospitality Practices
* Green Building and Property Management
* Corporate Environmental Policies
* ESG Awareness
* Green Workplace Certification

4. Hybrid Business Model

A. Individual Course Purchase
Allow companies or individuals to purchase a course once.

Examples:

* Basic Waste Sorting Course
* Recycling Awareness Course
* Sustainability Essentials Course

Each purchase includes:

* Online access
* Downloadable resources
* Quiz assessments
* Certificate of completion

B. Corporate Annual Subscription Packages

Starter Package

* Up to 10 employees

Business Package

* Up to 50 employees

Corporate Package

* Up to 200 employees

Enterprise Package

* Unlimited employees
* Custom pricing

Subscription benefits:

* Unlimited access to all courses
* Add new employees at any time
* Access to future course updates
* Unlimited certificates
* Company training dashboard
* Annual compliance reports

5. Learning Management System (LMS)

Each company account must include:

* Company profile
* Employee management
* Add/remove employees
* Training assignments
* Progress tracking
* Completion monitoring
* Quiz scores
* Certification records

6. HR and Management Dashboard

Managers can:

* View employee training status
* Track completion rates
* Generate training reports
* Export reports to PDF and Excel
* Identify employees requiring retraining
* Monitor onboarding training completion

7. Course Player

Users can:

* Watch training videos online
* Download PDF materials
* Complete quizzes
* Save progress automatically
* Resume training from any device

8. Certification System

Generate professional certificates automatically:

* Employee name
* Company name
* Course completed
* Date completed
* Unique certificate number
* QR code verification
* Downloadable PDF

9. Administration Panel

Administrator can:

* Create and edit courses
* Upload videos
* Upload documents
* Create quizzes
* Manage companies
* Manage subscriptions
* Track sales
* View analytics
* Monitor course performance

10. Compliance and ESG Reporting

Create a dedicated reporting module that allows companies to:

* Demonstrate employee environmental training completion
* Generate ESG-support reports
* Generate sustainability training summaries
* Produce audit-ready documentation
* Download annual environmental training reports

11. Marketing Features

Include:

* Testimonials
* Success stories
* FAQ section
* Blog about recycling and sustainability in Mauritius
* Lead capture forms
* Newsletter signup
* Free demo request form

12. Payment System

Support:

* Stripe
* Credit cards
* Subscription billing
* One-time purchases
* Automatic renewal management
* Invoice generation

13. Technical Requirements

* Mobile responsive design
* Tablet responsive design
* Desktop responsive design
* Secure authentication
* User roles and permissions
* SEO optimization
* Fast loading speed
* Professional UI/UX
* Green and blue environmental branding

14. Future AI Features

Design the architecture to support future additions:

* AI-generated quizzes
* AI tutor for learners
* Personalized learning paths
* AI-generated sustainability recommendations
* Automated compliance reminders

15. Multilingual Support

Prepare the platform for:

* English
* French
* Future Creole language support

16. Overall Design Inspiration

The platform should feel as professional as Udemy, Coursera, or LinkedIn Learning, but specialized for environmental training, waste management, sustainability, and ESG compliance in Mauritius.

The platform must be scalable and capable of serving thousands of users across multiple companies simultaneously.
